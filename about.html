Our Mission

We are an alliance of individuals and organizations united for abundant and equitable housing for a thriving Fullerton.

Our Vision

Building a sustainable thriving future for Fullerton through housing.

Our Goals

  • 1. Changing policy to increase affordable housing
  • 2. Building affordable housing on church land
  • 3. Creating an inclusionary housing fund
  • 4. Educating - demystifying homelessness; understanding the history and story of how we got to this place today

Our History

After years of creating programs to assist with homelessness, OC United and the city of Fullerton initiated a task force to find solutions to the homeless crisis. The task force discovered that homelessness cannot be solved with programs, rather the solution required that homelessness prevention through affordable housing was the best solution. A small group of pastors and community leaders gathered and met with Making Housing and Community Happen in Pasadena to learn more how to create a sustainable solution.

In 2023, the group sponsored a one-day seminar bringing key leaders from Southern California to discuss the causes of homelessness and the need for affordable housing. At the end of the seminar, the attendees decided to gather regularly to help advocate for affordable housing. This group is now called Fullerton Housing Alliance.
